About ZenTime

Our story, mission, and the team behind the meditation app

Our Story

ZenTime was created with a simple mission: to help people find moments of peace and clarity in their busy lives. In today's fast-paced world, taking time for mental wellness is more important than ever.

Our journey began in 2020 when our founder, after experiencing burnout from a demanding tech career, discovered the transformative power of meditation and mindfulness practices. Realizing how these techniques could benefit others facing similar challenges, the idea for ZenTime was born.

We combined ancient meditation techniques with modern focus strategies to create a comprehensive tool for mindfulness. Whether you're looking to reduce stress, improve concentration, or simply find a moment of tranquility, ZenTime provides the guidance and tools you need.

Our Mission

Bringing mindfulness and focus to everyone, everywhere

Accessibility

We believe mindfulness should be accessible to everyone. Our app is designed to be intuitive for beginners while offering depth for experienced practitioners.

Education

We're committed to educating users about the science and benefits of mindfulness, helping them understand how and why these practices work.

Community

We're building a supportive community of mindfulness practitioners who can share experiences and encourage each other on their journeys.
